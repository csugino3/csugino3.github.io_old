Active Metamaterials and Metastructures

Metamaterials are architected structures with small-scale (sub-wavelength) features that exhibit exotic effective properties, such as negative dynamic mass or negative dynamic stiffness. These exotic properties dramatically change the ways that energy propagates through the metamaterial, allowing waves to be blocked, filtered, in a designed way. Our research aims to develop active, intelligent metamaterial systems with fully reconfigurable effective properties.
